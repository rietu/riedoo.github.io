beabadoobee

Musician | Singer | Songwriter

Beatrice Kristi Ilejay Laus (born 3 June 2000), known professionally as Beabadoobee (bee-b…ô-DOO-bee), is a Filipino-English singer and songwriter.

She released five extended plays under the independent label Dirty Hit: Lice (2018), Patched Up (2018), Loveworm (2019), Space Cadet (2019) and Our Extended Play (2021). Her debut studio album, Fake It Flowers, was released in October 2020 and received critical acclaim. Her second studio album, Beatopia, was released on July 15, 2022.
